## Description

Turn your photo library into a beautiful map wallpaper. Cartogram reads the GPS locations from your photos and renders a heatmap overlay on a stylish vector map, showing where you've been right on your iPhone.

Download Cartogram for free and start with two hand-crafted themes: Cyberpunk and Midnight. Unlock Cartogram Pro with a one-time purchase to get all five themes, HDR export, and extra zoom for street-level detail.

Features:
- Generates wallpapers from your geotagged photo locations
- Free tier includes two themes: Cyberpunk and Midnight
- Cartogram Pro unlocks Ember, Ghost, and Moss
- Free zoom levels 10-14, with Pro zoom levels 15-16
- HDR export available with Cartogram Pro
- Auto-detects your current location or set any place manually
- Save generated wallpapers directly to Photos
- Privacy-first: all processing happens on-device, no data leaves your phone
- No account required, no tracking, no ads

iOS: Full-screen preview with pan, zoom, and rotation.

Map data from OpenStreetMap contributors, rendered with OpenFreeMap.

### Privacy Policy Content (draft):
Cartogram accesses your photo library solely to read GPS metadata from your images. No photos are uploaded, stored externally, or shared. Location data is used only to center the map and is not transmitted. Cartogram does not collect any personal data, does not use analytics, and does not contain advertising. All processing happens entirely on your device.

## App Review Notes
Cartogram requires Photos access to read GPS coordinates from geotagged photos. It also optionally uses Location Services to auto-center the map. Existing users who previously bought the paid app retain Pro access automatically through legacy migration checks. No network calls are made except to fetch map tiles from OpenFreeMap (tiles.openfreemap.org). No user data is collected or transmitted.
