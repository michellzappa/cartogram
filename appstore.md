## Description

Turn your photo library into a beautiful map wallpaper. Cartogram reads the GPS locations from your photos and renders a heatmap overlay on a stylish vector map — showing where you've been, right on your desktop or lock screen.

Choose from five hand-crafted themes — dark navy, charcoal, warm brown, light grey, or olive green — each paired with a complementary heatmap accent color. Every wallpaper is unique to your life.

Features:
- Generates wallpapers from your geotagged photo locations
- Five monochromatic map themes with matching heatmap accents
- Adjustable zoom level (10-16)
- Auto-detects your current location or set any place manually
- Save as image or set directly as wallpaper (macOS)
- Privacy-first: all processing happens on-device, no data leaves your phone or Mac
- No account required, no tracking, no ads

macOS: Lives in your menu bar for quick access.
iOS: Full-screen preview with pan, zoom, and rotation.

Map data from OpenStreetMap contributors, rendered with OpenFreeMap.

### Privacy Policy Content (draft):
Cartogram accesses your photo library solely to read GPS metadata from your images. No photos are uploaded, stored externally, or shared. Location data is used only to center the map and is not transmitted. Cartogram does not collect any personal data, does not use analytics, and does not contain advertising. ## App Review Notes
Cartogram requires Photos access to read GPS coordinates from geotagged photos. It also optionally uses Location Services to auto-center the map. No network calls are made except to fetch map tiles from OpenFreeMap (tiles.openfreemap.org). No user data is collected or transmitted.
